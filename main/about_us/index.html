ONGOING PROJECTS

THIRUMATHIKART


Thirumathikart is an e-commerce platform supporting self-help groups. It offers a curated collection of handmade products, including textiles, jewelry, and home decor. The platform connects artisans directly with customers, providing a secure and convenient marketplace.

This project is sponsored by Department of Science and Technology and mentored by Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The tech stack used here include Flutter, GoLang, PostgreSQL etc

For further details, visit Thirumathikart

ANIMAL DETECTION PROJECT


Animal detection project aims to prevent accidents caused by animals and facilitate safer coexistence between humans and animals by leveraging image processing.

This project utilizes YOLO model and image processing techniques to detect and recognize animals. Upon analyzing the presence of an animal and its proximity with respect to the human, an alarm turns on to alert the human about the presence of the animal.

RECENT WORKSHOPS

DATA SCIENCE WORKSHOP


On March 5, 2023, a data science workshop was held as part of Vortex, the CSE department's symposium, for students from all batches. The workshop covered essential aspects of data science, including data collection, preprocessing, exploratory analysis, machine learning, and visualization. It emphasized ethics and offered practical skills for academic and professional growth, culminating in attendees receiving certificates for their dedication to enhancing their data science expertise.

FUNDAMENTALS OF DEEP LEARNING WORKSHOP


An extensive workshop on 'The Fundamentals of Deep Learning' took place on April 14, 2023, for students from various years. The workshop covered key concepts such as deep learning principles, neural networks, training and optimization techniques, as well as specific applications like CNNs and RNNs for image analysis and NLP. Participants received certificates upon completion of the workshop.
